Pin Ally

Pin Ally helps you remember the places that matter.

Save locations quickly, organize them your way, and always know where to find them again. Whether it's a great restaurant, a hidden trail, or a spot you want to revisit, Pin Ally keeps everything in one simple, personal map.

Key Features:

  • Save locations in seconds. Drop a pin, add notes, and photos
  • Easily navigate to your pins using built in integration with Apple Maps
  • Clean and simple design, just your places and notes.
  • Built for you. Your data stays yours, stored on your device
  • Share pins with other Pin Ally users


Use Pin Ally for:
  • Travel planning
  • Saving local and out of town spots to visit or re-visit
  • Remember meaningful locations
  • Creating tags (categories) based on how you work and play (fishing locations, restaurants, hikes, photo locations, etc.)
  • Sharing pins with other Pin Ally users using standard iOS sharing functionality.
