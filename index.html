Pin Ally

Pin Ally helps you remember the places that matter.

Save locations quickly, organize them your way, and always know where to find them again. Whether it’s a great restaurant, a hidden trail, or a spot you want to revisit, Pin Ally keeps everything in one simple, personal map.

Key Features

  • Save locations in seconds with notes and photos.
  • Organize places with tags that fit how you work and explore.
  • Navigate to saved places using built-in Apple Maps integration.
  • Keep your information personal and under your control.
  • Share pins when you choose using standard iOS sharing.

Great For

  • Travel planning
  • Saving favorite local and out-of-town places
  • Remembering meaningful locations
  • Creating custom categories like hikes, restaurants, fishing spots, and photo locations
  • Sharing saved places with others when you want to
