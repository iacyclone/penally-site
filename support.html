Pin Ally Support

Need help with Pin Ally? If you have a question, found a bug, or want to request a feature, please use the support page below.

Open a Support Request

What to Include

Please include helpful details such as:

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • What you were doing when the issue happened
  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened

Privacy Note

Support requests submitted through GitHub Issues are public. Please do not include sensitive personal information, private location details, or anything you do not want publicly visible.

About Pin Ally

Pin Ally helps you save, organize, and revisit memorable places with tags, notes, and photos.
